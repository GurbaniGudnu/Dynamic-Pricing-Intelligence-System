## 📌 Overview

Dynamic Pricing Intelligence System is a business analytics project developed to analyze sales performance, competitor pricing, inventory management, and regional business trends through interactive Power BI dashboards.

The project follows a complete real-world analytics workflow starting from raw SQL tables to data modeling, DAX calculations, KPI reporting, and business intelligence dashboards.

The system helps businesses monitor:
- Revenue & profit trends
- Product performance
- Competitor pricing analysis
- Inventory risks
- Regional sales performance
- Business growth insights
